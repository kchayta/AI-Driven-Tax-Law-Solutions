When is the quarterly VAT return due in the UK?
In the UK, VAT returns are typically due one month and seven days after the end of the accounting period. For example:
  • Q1 (Jan-Mar): Due May 7
  • Q2 (Apr-Jun): Due August 7
  • Q3 (Jul-Sep): Due November 7
  • Q4 (Oct-Dec): Due February 7
What business expenses are tax deductible?
Common deductible business expenses include:
  • Office supplies and equipment
  • Employee salaries and benefits
  • Rent for business premises
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Professional services (legal, accounting)
  • Business travel and meals*

*Deduction rules vary by jurisdiction
